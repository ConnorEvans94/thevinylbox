Slowhand by Eric Clapton. This is Eric Clapton's fifth studion album, release on 25th November 1977 by RSO records. It was recorded at the Olmpic Studio's in London where Eric spent most of his time intoxicated. The album was produced by Glyn Johns. The album was named after Eric Clapton's nickname. Design and art direction: Eric Clapton with help from Pattie Boyd and Dave Stewart for 'El & Nell Ink'. Eric Clapton: guitar and vocals, Dick Sims: Keyboard, Marcy Levy: vocals, Carl Radle: bass guitar. Yvonne Elliman: Vocals, George Terry: guitar, Jamie Oldaker: drums and percussion, Mel Collins: saxophone.
