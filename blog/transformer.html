Transformer by Lou Reed from the velvet underground. Transformer is Lou Reed's second solo album after his self titled album earlier in the year. Transformer was released on 8th November 1972 by RCA records. Lou Reed wrote all the songs on the album although some were from his former band. Produced by David Bowie and Mick Ronson, song arrangements by Lou Reed, David Bowie and Mick Ronson. Backing vocals include: David Bowie, Mick Ronson and The Thunder Thighs. Guitars include: Lou Reed and Mick Ronson. Bass guitars include: Klaus Voorman and Herbie Flowers. Drums include: John Halzey(island records), Barry Desouza, Ritchie Dharma. Tuba: Herbie Flowers, baritone sax: Ronnie Ross, piano and recorders: Mick Ronson.
